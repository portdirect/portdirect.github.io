about us

Port.direct enables people to utilise computing resources more efficiently and effectively, helping you achieve your business goals. Business relies on computing to power everything from payment processing to the way we communicate with each other. port.direct, and the Harbor Platform, enable companies not only to be able to adapt to technological advances, but also to embrace and capitalise on them, gaining flexibility and agility leading to competitive advantages.

port.direct and the Harbor platform bring together the leading technologies built to address the needs and desires of IT users today, and lead the way into the computing of tomorrow. They remove the pain and cost associated with deploying, managing and updating IT infrastructure at every level from hardware and virtualisation to software and user management. By bringing together OpenStack, the leading open-source cloud platform, and the leading container management solutions they provide you with the tools to grow and adapt to an ever changing market:

Instant Self Service
access intuitive powerful self-service cloud computing services with nothing more than a browser.
On-Demand Scale
scale up and down based on demand or budget. Provision new infrastructure in minutes with no upfront cost or capacity commitments.
Flexible Resources
seamlessly combine multiple sites and servers into a scalable unified API driven computing resource.
Testing & Development
environments to test, develop and deploy your existing and new applications, accurately and efficiently.
App Hosting
develop, test and host next-generation and mobile applications. Run your apps in any combination of onsite or offsite.
Value and Assurance
